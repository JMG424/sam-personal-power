A simple map of the work

The work moves through four stages. Most people recognize themselves in one of them the moment they read it. The map isn't a label — it's an orientation.

01 Stage

The Block

Where the pattern becomes visible — the place you keep circling, even when you know better.

What helps now: seeing it clearly, without judgment.

02 Stage

The Clearing

Where the root cause and the emotional charge underneath it begin to release.

What helps now: presence, not pressure.

03 Stage

How to prepare

  • Be in a quiet, private space.
  • Have water nearby.
  • No driving or multitasking.
  • Leave a few minutes after to integrate.
  • Come open — there's nothing to perform.

After the session

  • You may feel tired or want quiet — that's the work integrating.
  • New insights often arrive in the hours and days after.
  • Old patterns become easier to notice and choose differently around.
  • Many people feel lighter or more spacious.
  • Rest, water, grounding, journaling, or a walk all help.
