## Brand rules for every post

**Tone:** warm, grounded, direct, never hypey. No "manifestation" jargon. No emojis in captions except sparingly: 🕯️ ✦ when it serves the brand. Spiritual *and* practical — never one without the other.

**Visual aesthetic:**
- Warm parchment (#FBF8F3) backgrounds for graphics
- Bronze gradient accents (#8B5A2B → #C28A4A) — sparingly
- Serif headlines: **Cormorant Garamond**, italic accent on the key word
- Body: clean sans (Inter)
- Brand portraits: golden-hour outdoor + warm-indoor (both already in `SAM ASSETS`)
- B-roll motifs: hands resting, single candle, water poured, journal open, lake at sunset, wind in linen
- **No stock wellness clichés** (no crystals, no chakras, no purple cosmic neon)

**Pacing rule:** every video — even the punchy ones — has at least one breath of silence. Sam's brand is the opposite of fast.

**Caption template (most posts):**
```
[1-2 line hook in plain language]

[3-5 lines that resonate or reframe]

[Single sentence invitation]

samspade.example  ·  First session $42
```

## Production tips (read before shooting)

1. **Two looks, used consistently.** The outdoor lake/golden hour for "movement and arrival" posts (1, 2, 3, 9). The warm indoor table for "intimacy and teaching" posts (5, 7, 10). Don't mix mid-post.
2. **Audio matters more than video.** Get a decent lavalier. Soft room. No reverb. Sam's voice is the brand.
3. **Hook discipline.** Every video earns its hook in the first 3 seconds. If you can't say the most important sentence in 3 seconds, rewrite.
4. **Captions on by default.** TikTok and IG Reels: hardcoded captions in serif italic for emphasis lines.
5. **No music with lyrics.** Soft ambient piano, or silence. Lyrics compete with Sam.
6. **Post cadence.** 3–4 posts/week. One long-form (carousel or YouTube) + two short-form (Reel/TikTok). Repurpose ruthlessly — every long-form should make 2–3 shorts.
7. **What to never post.** No promises of miracles. No income claims. No "this changed my life" testimonials without explicit written permission. No before/after framing.
8. **Reply discipline.** When prospects DM ("is this for me?"), reply with one question, not a pitch: *"What's the goal that hasn't moved?"* Then book the $42 first session.
