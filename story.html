The Story

Abigail's Light

The story behind the work — how Sam was sent back, what Abigail taught him, and the lineage he carries forward.

✦

There are moments that stop time.

For Samuel Spade, it was a heart attack.

In that space between here and gone, something cracked open. It wasn't an ending — it was an initiation. A divine summons to a path that had been waiting his entire life. In that stillness, the light didn't just visit. It stayed.

One year before, his mentor Abigail had given him one of the greatest gifts a soul can receive: she taught him how to channel without being consumed. How to hold the light — the love, the gratitude, the joy — without losing himself in it.

The channel must be tended with care. Abigail taught him that holding the light is both a gift and a responsibility — to stay grounded, clear, and rooted in Love, Gratitude & Joy, so that what flows through remains pure, and every soul that enters this space is held safely in the light.

Carrying an unbroken lineage of light — and trained in the transformational tools of the Compassion Key by Edward Mannix — Samuel now does what he was sent back to do.

He helps souls clear their blockages — the patterns that have kept them from the life they came here to live — connecting them to their higher self so the light can move freely. As above, so below. From the infinite to you. From you back to the infinite.

You don't need a heart attack to wake up.
You just need a guide.
